NEP CHALLENGE 2021

OVERVIEW

Gopali Youth Welfare Society believes that students have a strong potential to bring a change in society. GYWS has provided a space for youth across India with the help of the LiGHT(Living in Greater Horizons Together) initiative which provides a platform to the youth for grooming the society by conducting new events. The main aim of the event is to promote youth empowerment throughout the country. Therefore, expanding our reach and promoting social work. This competition will give students an opportunity to stretch their imagination and come up with innovative solutions, along with cash prizes for the winning teams.

Guidelines

  • Problem Statement will be sent to the participants a week prior via mail.
  • The participants can view the National Education Policy released by the government to get a better grasp on the problem.

  • This case study would go in three-phase :

    1. Extract Submission
    2. Presentation Submission
    3. Panel Presentation

  • Participants will be judged on the Quality of Analysis, Creativity, Feasibility of the solution, the overall presentation of the case.

Eligibility




Enthusiastic Participation of any college student who is willing to bring up a change in the method of learning according to the National Education Policy are welcome.



In each team, there should be 3-5 members and all the members of the team should be from one college only.



Students from any institute irrespective of department or field of study can come forward to participate.

Important Dates and deadlines(Timeline)

  • Pre-launch along with Problem Statement release is on 7th Feb'21.
  • Extract submission for round-1 is before 14th Feb'21.
  • Results of the first round will be out on 21st Feb’21.
  • Present submission for round-2 is on 28th Feb’21.
  • Results of the second round will be out between March1-March-3.
  • The final round is on 7th March’21.

STAGES

  • Round - 1: Extract Submission

    1. The case will be circulated to all participants after registration individually through the mail.
    2. The participating teams have to submit a comprehensive abstract consisting of 3-5 pages (in pdf/.docx format) before the abstract submission deadline.

  • Round - 2: Presentation Submission

    1. 50 teams will qualify for round 2.
    2. A detailed approach and strategy implementation should be prepared by the selected teams in ppt format. (maximum of 10 slides).
    3. The participants have to submit both the .pdf and .pptx form of the presentation by 9th Feb’21.

  • Round - 3: Panel Presentation

    1. A total of 10 teams will be selected for the final round.
    2. The team has to present its submitted ppt in front of our Judge.
    3. Round 3 will be conducted on 21st Feb’21.

What’s a stake - Rewards and Prizes

  • Winners of the competition will be receiving Cash Prizes of Rs. 40,000/-
  • 1st Runner up of the competition will be receiving a Cash Prize of Rs. 15,000/-
  • 2nd Runner up of the competition will be receiving a Cash Prize of Rs. 5,000/-
